Who this app is for Section 1

MY Food Cart Inventory is designed for business use by authorized users (e.g., manager/operations staff). It is not intended for children.

Information we collect Section 2

The app may collect and store the following, depending on how you use it:

  • Account & profile info: user name (if provided), role, and access PIN (for app login).
  • Business data you enter: inventory items, quantities, pricing, sales records, expense records, summaries.
  • Optional media: profile photo (only if you choose to add one).
Permissions: If the app requests camera/photos, it is only for features you actively use (e.g., profile photo). You can deny permissions and continue using core features.

How we use information Section 3

  • Provide app functionality (inventory, sales, expenses, summaries).
  • Authenticate users and enforce roles/permissions.
  • Support offline usage by storing data locally on your device.
  • Synchronize data to your cloud database when sync is enabled.

Sync & cloud storage (Supabase) Section 4

If you configure cloud sync, the app transmits your business data to your Supabase backend (database and storage) so it can be backed up and shared across devices.

Important: Your Supabase project configuration (access rules, backups, and user management) affects how synced data is protected. Keep your admin credentials secure.

Sharing of information Section 5

We do not sell your data.

We share data only:

  • With your configured Supabase backend for syncing (if enabled by you), and
  • When required to comply with applicable law.

Data retention Section 6

  • Local data: remains on your device until you delete it, reset the app, or uninstall the app.
  • Synced data: remains in your Supabase project until you delete it from your database/storage.

Security Section 7

  • Data can be stored locally for offline use.
  • We use reasonable measures to protect data, but no system is 100% secure.
  • You are responsible for securing devices that can access the app (device lock, PIN, etc.).

Your choices Section 8

  • You can skip optional fields (like profile photo or optional details).
  • You can edit/delete business entries in the app.
  • To remove synced data, delete it from your Supabase project.
